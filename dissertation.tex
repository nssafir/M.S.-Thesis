%--------------------------ABSTRACT---------------------------%
\input{metafiles/abstractpage.tex}
\doublespacing{\makeabstract{%
Metric learning methods generally do not incorporate unlabelled data. We propose performing deep metric learning (DML) on the latent space of the variational autoencoder (VAE) so that the DML task can be jointly optimized with the VAE to incorporate unlabelled data. We test this approach across recent metric losses and experimentally evaluate two atomic benefits to the performing DML on the VAE latent space: the enhanced ability to train using unlabelled data and to induce bias given prior knowledge.
}}%
